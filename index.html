About

Turkey Music is a simple music player built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It allows users to upload their own music files and create playlists. The player features basic controls such as play, pause, and volume adjustment. The design is clean and user-friendly, making it easy for anyone to enjoy their music collection.
